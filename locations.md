## United States

### New York City, New York

New York was central to Epstein's operations. His Upper East Side mansion at 9 East 71st Street served as a primary base for alleged crimes and was one of the largest private residences in Manhattan.

- **[Jeffrey Epstein](Details/Jeffrey_Epstein.md)** (2019, age 66) — **HIGHLY SUSPICIOUS.** Found hanging in his cell at the Metropolitan Correctional Center. Guards asleep, cameras failed, brother says murder. 2026 DOJ files show unidentified figure near cell hours before death.
- **[Ruslana Korshunova](Details/Ruslana_Korshunova.md)** (2008, age 20) — **SUSPICIOUS.** Fell from ninth-floor apartment balcony. Documented on Epstein's jet to his island at 18. Body landed 8.5m from building. No suicide note, no drugs/alcohol. Mother rejects suicide ruling.
- **[Kate Spade](Details/Kate_Spade.md)** (2018, age 55) — **UNCERTAIN.** Fashion designer found hanged in Park Avenue apartment. Died 3 days before Anthony Bourdain. No verified Epstein connection. History of depression.
- **[Vitaly Churkin](Details/Vitaly_Churkin.md)** (2017, age 64) — **UNCERTAIN.** Russian UN Ambassador who suffered sudden cardiac event in Manhattan. Cause of death initially withheld at State Department request.

### West Palm Beach, Florida

Two Epstein victims died of overdoses in hotel rooms in West Palm Beach — the same area where Epstein's Palm Beach mansion was located.

- **[Leigh Skye Patrick](Details/Leigh_Skye_Patrick.md)** (2017, age 29) — Likely trauma-related. Accidental heroin overdose. Lured to Epstein's Palm Beach mansion at age 16 in 2003. Family says addiction began from Epstein's abuse.
- **[Carolyn Andriano](Details/Carolyn_Andriano.md)** (2023, age 36) — **MODERATE SUSPICION.** Accidental overdose. Testified against Maxwell in 2021. Mother says "doesn't make sense" — was reportedly drug-free and buying property to start a new life.

### Palm Beach, Florida

Epstein's Palm Beach mansion was a primary location for alleged trafficking. The Palm Beach Police Department investigation (2005–2008) was one of the first major law enforcement efforts against Epstein.

- **[Joe Recarey](Details/Joe_Recarey.md)** (2018, age 50) — **MODERATE SUSPICION.** Lead detective on the 2005–2008 Epstein investigation. Died unexpectedly of a "brief illness." Had over 150 commendations. Died a year before Epstein's re-arrest.

### Coral Gables, Florida

- **[Roy Black](Details/Roy_Black.md)** (2025, age 80) — Likely old age. Died at home. Epstein defense attorney. Age 80, battling serious illness. Family says unrelated.

### Miami, Florida

- **[Ronald R. Eppinger Sr.](Details/Ronald_Eppinger.md)** (2006, age 80) — Unknown. Allegedly first to traffic Virginia Giuffre to Epstein. Cause of death not documented.

### Los Angeles, California

- **[Steve Bing](Details/Steve_Bing.md)** (2020, age 55) — **MODERATE SUSPICION.** Fell from 27th floor. Major Clinton donor named in 2026 Epstein files. Had documented depression.
- **[Michael Jackson](Details/Michael_Jackson.md)** (2009, age 50) — Not suspicious. Died of acute drug intoxication caused by personal physician. Visited Epstein's Palm Beach mansion once in 2003. Unrelated to Epstein.
- **[Anne Heche](Details/Anne_Heche.md)** (2022, age 53) — **UNCERTAIN.** Actress died in fiery car crash in Mar Vista. Claimed to be promoting a trafficking film. Toxicology showed cocaine/fentanyl. Coincided with 2022 death cluster.

### Malibu, California

- **[Thomas Bowers](Details/Thomas_Bowers.md)** (2019, age 55) — **SUSPICIOUS.** Head of Deutsche Bank wealth management that moved Epstein's money. Found hanged at home 3 months after Epstein. Same method as Epstein and Brunel.

### Palos Verdes Estates, California

- **[Chester Bennington](Details/Chester_Bennington.md)** (2017, age 41) — **SUSPICIOUS.** Linkin Park vocalist found hanged. Close friend of Chris Cornell. Died on Cornell's birthday, same method, two months later. Linked to "Silent Children" documentary.

### Valencia (Santa Clarita), California

## Geographic Patterns

- **California dominance**: Seven deaths across Los Angeles, Malibu, Palos Verdes Estates, and Santa Clarita — the largest concentration in any single state.
- **Florida concentration**: Five deaths in the Palm Beach / Miami / Coral Gables area — the epicenter of Epstein's crimes and the original investigation.
- **Victim deaths in Palm Beach hotels**: Both Leigh Skye Patrick (2017) and Carolyn Andriano (2023) died of overdoses in hotel rooms in West Palm Beach.
- **2017–2018 cluster spans 4 countries**: Chris Cornell (Detroit), Chester Bennington (California), Avicii (Oman), Kate Spade (New York), and Anthony Bourdain (France) — five deaths in 13 months across the US, France, and Oman.
- **Balcony/building falls span 4 countries**: Korshunova (New York), Drozdova (Kyiv), Wendy Leigh (London), and Steve Bing (Los Angeles).
- **Prison deaths**: Three individuals died in custody or prison settings (Epstein, Brunel, Kessler), two by hanging.
- **Sealed records**: Mark Middleton's death in rural Arkansas had records sealed by Judge Alice Gray — unusual for a suicide ruling.
- **International reach**: Deaths span 7 countries (USA, France, UK, Ukraine, Oman, Australia, and the unverified Zorro Ranch allegations), reflecting the global scope of Epstein's network.
