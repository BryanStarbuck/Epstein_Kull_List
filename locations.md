## United States

### New York City, New York

New York was central to Epstein's operations. His Upper East Side mansion at 9 East 71st Street served as a primary base for alleged crimes and was one of the largest private residences in Manhattan.

- **[Jeffrey Epstein](Details/Jeffrey_Epstein.md)** (2019, age 66) — Found hanging in his cell at the Metropolitan Correctional Center on August 10, 2019. Guards falsified logs. Cameras malfunctioned. Brother says murder.
- **[Ruslana Korshunova](Details/Ruslana_Korshunova.md)** (2008, age ~21) — Kazakh model who jumped from her ninth-floor apartment balcony. Had visited Little St. James Island in 2006 at age 18, flying on the "Lolita Express" with Epstein and Sarah Kellen. Died 2 years later.

### West Palm Beach, Florida

Two Epstein victims died of overdoses in hotel rooms in West Palm Beach — the same area where Epstein's Palm Beach mansion was located.

- **[Leigh Skye Patrick](Details/Leigh_Skye_Patrick.md)** (2017, age 29) — Accidental heroin overdose. Lured to Epstein's Palm Beach mansion at age 16 in 2003. Family says addiction began from Epstein's abuse.
- **[Carolyn Andriano](Details/Carolyn_Andriano.md)** (2023, age 36) — Accidental overdose. Testified against Ghislaine Maxwell in 2021. Mother questioned circumstances. Left 5 children.

### Palm Beach, Florida

Epstein's Palm Beach mansion was a primary location for alleged trafficking. The Palm Beach Police Department investigation (2005–2008) was one of the first major law enforcement efforts against Epstein.

- **[Joe Recarey](Details/Joe_Recarey.md)** (2018, age 50) — Lead detective on the 2005–2008 Epstein investigation. Died of a "brief illness." Had over 150 commendations. Died a year before Epstein's re-arrest.

### Coral Gables, Florida

- **[Roy Black](Details/Roy_Black.md)** (2025, age 80) — Died at home. Attorney who helped Epstein in the West Palm Beach case. Family says death unrelated to Epstein.

### Miami, Florida

- **[Ronald R. Eppinger Sr.](Details/Ronald_Eppinger.md)** (2006, age 80) — Co-conspirator who allegedly was the first to traffic Virginia Giuffre to Epstein. Cause of death unknown.

### Los Angeles, California

- **[Steve Bing](Details/Steve_Bing.md)** (2020, age 55) — Movie producer who fell from the 27th floor of his apartment building. Had spoken to the FBI about Epstein before his death.
- **[Michael Jackson](Details/Michael_Jackson.md)** (2009, age 50) — Died of acute drug intoxication caused by his personal physician (ruled homicide). Visited Epstein's Palm Beach mansion once in 2003. Death attributed to personal physician, not Epstein connection.

### Malibu, California

- **[Thomas Bowers](Details/Thomas_Bowers.md)** (2019, age 55) — Head of Deutsche Bank's wealth management division, which moved money for Epstein. Found hanged at his home. Died 3 months after Epstein.

### Derby, Connecticut

- **[Steven Hoffenberg](Details/Steven_Hoffenberg.md)** (2022, age 77) — Financier and Epstein's early mentor. Found dead in his apartment, body undiscovered for 7+ days. Had confessed about the Epstein-Maxwell blackmail operation and cooperated with the FBI.

### Boston, Massachusetts

- **[Marvin Minsky](Details/Marvin_Minsky.md)** (2016, age 88) — MIT AI pioneer who died of cerebral hemorrhage. Named in Epstein court filings. Received Epstein funding.

### Arkansas

- **[Mark Middleton](Details/Mark_Middleton.md)** (2022, age 59) — Former advisor to President Bill Clinton who admitted Epstein to the White House. Found hanged with a shotgun wound; shotgun found 30 feet from the body. Death records sealed by a judge.

### New Mexico

- **[Bill Richardson](Details/Bill_Richardson.md)** (2023, age 75) — Former UN ambassador and New Mexico governor. Died in his sleep. Named in Epstein documents. Met Epstein years after conviction. Governed the state where Zorro Ranch is located.

### Zorro Ranch, New Mexico

Epstein's 7,500-acre ranch near Stanley, New Mexico. Allegations from released DOJ files claim bodies may be buried on the property.

- **[Two Unnamed Foreign Women](Details/Zorro_Ranch_Unnamed_Victims.md)** (year unknown) — 2026 DOJ files contain an email claiming two foreign women were strangled and buried near the ranch. No bodies found. Completely unverified.

### Georgia (federal prison)

- **[Berry L. Kessler](Details/Berry_Kessler.md)** (2005, age 84) — Died in a Georgia federal prison. Prime suspect in the 1985 murder of Arthur Shapiro. Convicted of separate murder-for-hire plots. FBI docs link to Epstein.

## International

### Paris, France

Epstein maintained an apartment in Paris, and his associate Jean-Luc Brunel operated a modeling agency there.

- **[Jean-Luc Brunel](Details/Jean_Luc_Brunel.md)** (2022, age 75) — Head of a French modeling agency, found hanged in his Paris prison cell while awaiting trial for trafficking girls to Epstein. His death mirrored Epstein's own — hanging in a prison cell. Lawyers demanded a probe.

### London, United Kingdom

- **[Wendy Leigh](Details/Wendy_Leigh.md)** (2016, age 65) — Journalist and biographer found beneath a balcony. Was investigating Epstein. Her agent said her optimistic outlook contradicted the suicide explanation.

### Cambridge, United Kingdom

- **[Stephen Hawking](Details/Stephen_Hawking.md)** (2018, age 76) — Physicist who died of natural causes (ALS). Visited Epstein's island. Death clearly from longstanding illness.

### Neergabby, Western Australia

- **[Virginia Giuffre](Details/Virginia_Giuffre.md)** (2025, age 41) — The most prominent Epstein accuser, died by suicide. Had expressed safety fears in a 2019 court filing about retaliation from powerful figures connected to Epstein.

## Unknown Locations

The following deaths have no confirmed specific location in available records:

- **[Arthur Shapiro](Details/Arthur_Shapiro.md)** (1985, age 43) — Epstein's predecessor at L Brands, gunned down weeks before IRS questioning about illegal tax shelters. Unsolved murder. Epstein took his position after his death.
- **[Alfredo Rodriguez](Details/Alfredo_Rodriguez.md)** (2014, age 60) — Epstein's former house manager, died of mesothelioma. Possessed Epstein's "black book" (called "The Holy Grail"). Claimed he needed it as life insurance.
- **[Efrain "Stone" Reyes](Details/Efrain_Stone_Reyes.md)** (2020, age 51) — Epstein's last cellmate, died of COVID-19 weeks after talking with federal investigators. Had been moved from Epstein's cell 1 day before Epstein's death.
- **[Kenneth Starr](Details/Kenneth_Starr.md)** (2022, age 76) — Attorney who helped Epstein get the 2008 sweetheart plea deal. Died of surgical complications.
- **[John Connolly](Details/John_Connolly.md)** (2022, age 78) — Former NYPD detective and journalist. Co-authored *Filthy Rich*. Appeared in *Who Killed Jeffrey Epstein?* documentary. Died of brief illness.

## Geographic Patterns

- **Florida concentration**: Five deaths in the Palm Beach / Miami / Coral Gables area — the epicenter of Epstein's crimes and the original investigation.
- **Victim deaths in Palm Beach hotels**: Both Leigh Skye Patrick (2017) and Carolyn Andriano (2023) died of overdoses in hotel rooms in West Palm Beach.
- **Prison deaths**: Three individuals died in custody or prison settings (Epstein, Brunel, Kessler), two by hanging.
- **Sealed records**: Mark Middleton's death in rural Arkansas had records sealed by a judge — unusual for a suicide ruling.
- **International reach**: Deaths span from Australia to France to the UK, reflecting the global scope of Epstein's network.
- **Cluster around Epstein properties**: Deaths concentrate near New York, Florida, and California — areas where Epstein had residences or strong connections.
