# Epstein-Related Deaths Research Database

This directory contains comprehensive research into conspiracy theories and claims about deaths allegedly connected to Jeffrey Epstein. The research was conducted through systematic web searches organized by year, location, and person categories.

## Files in This Directory

### Main Database
- **epstein_deaths.yaml** - Primary database containing 22+ individuals with structured information including:
  - Name
  - Location of death
  - Year of death
  - Cause of death
  - Person type/category
  - Multiple sources with URLs, titles, descriptions, and summaries

### Category Files
- **person_types.csv** - 30 categories of people potentially connected to Epstein case:
  - Women, Men, Underage Victims
  - Law Enforcement, Politicians, Co-conspirators
  - Witnesses, Journalists, Lawyers, Judges
  - Prison Guards, Pilots, Staff Members
  - Business Associates, Intelligence Operatives
  - And 15 more categories

- **locations.csv** - 40 locations where Epstein-related deaths occurred or were investigated:
  - States: New York, Florida, New Mexico, California, Ohio, Virgin Islands
  - Countries: France, United Kingdom, Israel, Morocco, Spain, Monaco, Switzerland, Austria, Belgium
  - Cities: Manhattan, Palm Beach, Santa Fe, Los Angeles, Miami, Paris, London
  - Categories: Private Islands, Luxury Resorts, Mansions, Prisons, Universities

### Research Findings
- **findings_timesnow.txt** - Detailed notes from Times Now article about mysterious deaths
- **findings_uinterview_22_deaths.txt** - Complete list from Uinterview article documenting 22 deaths
- **findings_zorro_ranch.txt** - Information about alleged deaths at Zorro Ranch, New Mexico
