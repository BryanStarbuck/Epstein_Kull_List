# Andrew Breitbart
Conservative media founder died of heart attack in 2012, weeks before releasing exposing videos; linked to Clinton/Weiner circles.

## Basic Information
- **Full Name:** Andrew James Breitbart
- **Date of Death:** March 1, 2012
- **Age at Death:** 43
- **Cause of Death:** Heart failure (hypertrophic cardiomyopathy and dilated cardiomyopathy)
- **Location:** Brentwood, Los Angeles, California
- **Suspicion Level:** UNCERTAIN

## Connection to Epstein Network
Breitbart's connection to the Epstein network is indirect, running primarily through Anthony Weiner and broader "Clinton body count" narratives. In the weeks before his death, Breitbart had been teasing the release of explosive videos, including footage he claimed would damage Barack Obama and potentially expose figures in the Clinton/Weiner political orbit. Some of this material indirectly fed into what later became the Pizzagate narrative.

His most direct link comes through Anthony Weiner — Breitbart broke the original Weiner sexting scandal in 2011, which eventually led to the discovery of Weiner's laptop in 2016. That laptop allegedly contained material that some claim connected to Epstein-adjacent networks (see [NYPD Weiner laptop deaths](./other/laptop.md)).

Breitbart famously tweeted in 2011: "How prog-guru John Podesta isn't household name as world class underage sex slave op cover-upperer defending unspeakable dregs is beyond me." This tweet, rediscovered after Pizzagate, fueled theories that Breitbart had knowledge of elite trafficking networks before his death.

## Circumstances Raising Suspicion
- Died suddenly at 43 while walking near his home, hours after telling a dinner companion he had explosive videos to release
- Was teasing a "Part 2" release of damaging political material at the time of death
- The coroner's office employee who handled his autopsy (Michael Cormier) died of suspected arsenic poisoning weeks later — this is unverified and disputed
- His 2011 tweet about John Podesta and "underage sex slave" cover-ups was seen as prescient after Pizzagate emerged
- History of confrontational public behavior but no widely known heart condition prior to death
- Autopsy confirmed natural causes (heart disease), and his father-in-law Orson Bean stated Breitbart had known heart problems

## Context
Andrew Breitbart was a conservative media entrepreneur and founder of Breitbart News. He was known for breaking major political stories, including the ACORN hidden camera videos and the Anthony Weiner sexting scandal. He was a combative public figure who regularly challenged mainstream media and Democratic Party figures.

On the evening of February 29, 2012, Breitbart collapsed while walking near his Brentwood home. He was pronounced dead at Ronald Reagan UCLA Medical Center in the early hours of March 1. The LA County Coroner ruled his death was due to heart failure from dilated cardiomyopathy with focal myocardial bridging and hypertrophic cardiomyopathy.

The "Part 2" videos Breitbart had been teasing turned out to be footage of Obama at Harvard with Derrick Bell, which was released after his death. Some theorists believe more damaging material existed that was never released.

The connection to Epstein-related networks is primarily through the Weiner/Clinton political orbit rather than a direct Epstein link. Breitbart's death is more commonly associated with broader political corruption theories ("Clinton body count") than specifically with child trafficking cover-ups, though his Podesta tweet blurs that line.
