## Context
Anne Heche was an actress known for roles in films and television spanning three decades. On August 5, 2022, she crashed her car into a two-story house in Mar Vista, Los Angeles, causing a massive fire. She was rescued from the burning vehicle but suffered severe burns and smoke inhalation. She was declared brain dead on August 11 and taken off life support on August 12.

Toxicology reports found cocaine and fentanyl in her system at the time of the crash. Her representatives initially denied drug use, but the coroner's report was definitive.

Heche had been open about being sexually abused by her father as a child and had spoken about the lasting trauma. She had a history of erratic public behavior attributed to mental health struggles stemming from that abuse.
