## Assessment: SUSPICIOUS

Yassenoff was a business partner of [Berry Kessler](Berry_Kessler.md) — the accountant who was the prime suspect in the 1985 murder of [Arthur Shapiro](Arthur_Shapiro.md) and who was later convicted of the 1991 murder-for-hire of [John Deroo](John_Deroo.md). Yassenoff, Shapiro, and Kessler were all "connected through a maze of business dealings" per the Columbus Dispatch, tied to the Les Wexner orbit that later enabled Epstein. Kessler came under suspicion but was never charged.

## Circumstances of Death

Frank Yassenoff, 64, and his fiancee [Ella Rich](Ella_Rich.md) were both found shot to death in Columbus, Ohio on March 23, 1970. The case remains unsolved. Berry Kessler came under suspicion but was never charged.

## Connection to the Epstein Network

Yassenoff was connected to the same Columbus, Ohio financial network that tied into Les Wexner's business empire. Kessler, Shapiro, and Yassenoff were all linked through overlapping business dealings. This network is the foundation upon which Epstein built his financial relationship with Wexner beginning in 1987.

The fact that Kessler — who was later convicted of arranging the murder of another business partner ([John Deroo](John_Deroo.md)) — was suspected in the Yassenoff killing suggests a pattern of eliminating business associates that may have extended to [Arthur Shapiro](Arthur_Shapiro.md).
