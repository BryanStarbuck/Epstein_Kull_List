## Circumstances of Death

Val Broeksmit was reported missing and subsequently found dead on the campus of Woodrow Wilson High School in the El Sereno neighborhood of Los Angeles on April 25, 2022. He appeared to have fallen from a tree, suffering blunt-force trauma, broken ribs, and a spinal fracture. The Los Angeles County coroner ruled the death an accident. Police said foul play was not suspected.

## Background

After his adoptive father [William Broeksmit](William_Broeksmit.md) was found hanged in London in January 2014, Val discovered thousands of confidential Deutsche Bank documents in his father's email account. He spent years reviewing these documents, which revealed details of the bank's internal operations, and provided them to:

- The FBI, becoming a formal informant
- Journalists, including those at The New York Times and BuzzFeed News
- Members of the U.S. Congress investigating Deutsche Bank

The documents Val uncovered were relevant to multiple federal investigations, including those into Deutsche Bank's relationships with clients like Jeffrey Epstein. Deutsche Bank later paid $150 million in fines for compliance failures related to Epstein's accounts.

Val had a history of personal struggles, including substance abuse, and had previously been arrested in Russia. However, he was actively cooperating with federal investigators at the time of his death.
