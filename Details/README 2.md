## Methodology

Research conducted February 7, 2026 through:
1. Web searches by year (2003-2025)
2. Web searches by location (40+ locations including New York, Florida, California, France, UK, New Mexico, etc.)
3. Web searches by person category (30+ categories)
4. Cross-referencing multiple sources
5. Documenting both conspiracy claims and official rulings

## Important Notes

1. **Trauma and Addiction**: Medical research confirms that survivors of sexual exploitation have significantly elevated risk of addiction and overdose. The victim deaths by overdose are consistent with this documented pattern.

2. **Natural Deaths**: Many individuals were elderly or had known health conditions. Deaths ruled natural by medical examiners should not be assumed suspicious without evidence.

3. **Unverified Claims**: The Zorro Ranch claims are completely unverified. No bodies have been found and no official investigation has confirmed these allegations.

4. **Conspiracy vs. Evidence**: This database documents what conspiracy theorists claim, not what evidence supports. Official investigations have not identified coordinated silencing.
