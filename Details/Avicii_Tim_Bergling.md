# Avicii (Tim Bergling)
DJ who depicted child trafficking in music, died at 28 by self-inflicted wounds in Oman.

## Basic Information
- **Full Name:** Tim Bergling (stage name: Avicii)
- **Date of Death:** April 20, 2018
- **Age at Death:** 28
- **Cause of Death:** Apparent suicide by self-inflicted wounds (sharp glass)
- **Location:** Muscat, Oman
- **Suspicion Level:** SUSPICIOUS

## Connection to Epstein Network
X users and online investigators claim Avicii was actively working on or tied to "The Silent Children," a documentary project exposing child sex trafficking and Hollywood/elite pedophile rings linked to Epstein. Posts group him with [Chris Cornell](Chris_Cornell.md), [Chester Bennington](Chester_Bennington.md), and [Anthony Bourdain](Anthony_Bourdain.md) as part of a team that "knew too much" about elite trafficking networks.

## Circumstances Raising Suspicion
- His death came amid the alleged development of "The Silent Children" documentary
- The documentary was reportedly shelved or canceled after his death and the deaths of others in the same circle
- Multiple high-profile figures tied to the same project died by "suicide" in a tight 2017–2018 window
- The unusual clustering of deaths among individuals allegedly connected to trafficking exposure efforts
- His music video for "For a Better Day" (2015) explicitly depicted child trafficking, leading many to believe he was personally invested in exposing these networks

## Context
Avicii was one of the world's most successful electronic music producers and DJs. His death at 28 was publicly attributed to struggles with mental health and physical health issues related to his demanding touring schedule. His family acknowledged he "could not go on any longer" and "wanted to find peace."

The "Silent Children" documentary connection has been repeatedly stated by its creators and independent fact-checkers to have had no involvement from Avicii, Cornell, Bennington, or Bourdain. However, X users treat the pattern of deaths as non-coincidental silencing of individuals who were exposing child trafficking networks overlapping with Epstein's operation.

## Part of the 2017–2018 Death Cluster

The clustering of these deaths, combined with the alleged trafficking documentary connection, is the primary driver of suspicion online.
