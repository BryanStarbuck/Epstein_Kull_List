## Circumstances of Death

Carolyn Andriano died on May 23, 2023, in a room at the DoubleTree by Hilton hotel in West Palm Beach, Florida. Toxicology reports showed the overdose involved a combination of methadone, fentanyl, and alprazolam — a dangerous combination. Police and toxicology reports classified the death as accidental.

Her death was not announced publicly until months later.
