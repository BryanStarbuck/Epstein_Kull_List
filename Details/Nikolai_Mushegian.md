# Nikolai Mushegian
MakerDAO co-founder and crypto pioneer who tweeted about a CIA/Mossad sex trafficking ring in Puerto Rico hours before drowning at age 29.

## Basic Information
- **Full Name:** Nikolai Arcadie Mushegian (also spelled Muchgian in official records)
- **Date of Birth:** March 28, 1993
- **Date of Death:** October 28, 2022
- **Age at Death:** 29
- **Cause of Death:** Drowning
- **Location:** Condado Beach, San Juan, Puerto Rico
- **Official Ruling:** Accidental drowning (riptide)
- **Suspicion Level:** SUSPICIOUS

## Connection to Epstein Network
Nikolai Mushegian was an American computer scientist and cryptocurrency pioneer who co-founded MakerDAO, designed the Single-Collateral Dai stablecoin system, co-authored the Balancer white paper, and co-founded the RAI stablecoin project. He was a graduate of Carnegie Mellon University's School of Computer Science (2014) and had donated ~$1.4 million in MKR tokens to his alma mater.

Mushegian had relocated to Puerto Rico — the same Caribbean island territory where [Jeffrey Epstein](Jeffrey_Epstein.md) maintained significant operations and where multiple Epstein associates relocated for tax advantages. Hours before his death, Mushegian tweeted allegations of a CIA and Mossad-run sex trafficking and blackmail operation based in Puerto Rico and the Caribbean islands — the same geographic region as Epstein's trafficking network centered on Little St. James island in the U.S. Virgin Islands.

His final tweet read: **"CIA and Mossad and pedo elite are running some kind of sex trafficking entrapment blackmail ring out of Puerto Rico and caribbean islands. They are going to frame me with a laptop planted by my ex gf who was a spy. They will torture me to death."**

Approximately four hours after posting this tweet, Mushegian was found dead in the ocean at Condado Beach.

## Circumstances Raising Suspicion
- Posted a detailed warning about CIA/Mossad sex trafficking blackmail ring in Puerto Rico and the Caribbean — the same region as Epstein's operations — just hours before his death
- Specifically predicted he would be killed: "They will torture me to death"
- Claimed his ex-girlfriend was a spy who would plant a laptop to frame him — suggesting intelligence agency involvement
- Was found fully clothed with his wallet still on him, which is unusual for someone who went swimming voluntarily
- Died at 29, young and in apparent good health
- Puerto Rico and the Caribbean islands were central to Epstein's trafficking network; Mushegian's tweet specifically called out these same locations
- The official explanation — caught in a riptide — was accepted quickly with no further investigation despite the extraordinary timing of his tweet
- His allegations of intelligence agency sex trafficking operations mirror what is known about [Jeffrey Epstein](Jeffrey_Epstein.md)'s network, which had documented ties to intelligence agencies through [Robert Maxwell](Robert_Maxwell.md) (Mossad) and others
- Autopsy found only a small skull laceration; no other signs of violence were reported

## Context
Mushegian was born in Lexington, Kentucky, to parents who had immigrated from the Soviet Union in 1991. He attended Blue Valley North High School in Overland Park, Kansas, and graduated from Carnegie Mellon University in 2014. He was recognized as a visionary in cryptocurrency, having helped create fundamental infrastructure for decentralized finance (DeFi).

His contributions included: co-founding MakerDAO and designing the DAI stablecoin (2017), developing Wrapped Ether (WETH, 2016), co-authoring the Balancer DEX white paper (2019), co-founding RAI stablecoin (2020), and co-founding RICO stablecoin (2022).

Police in Puerto Rico stated they found no evidence of foul play. However, the crypto community and independent researchers noted the extraordinary coincidence of his death occurring hours after his tweet warning of a CIA/Mossad trafficking ring — particularly given that the Caribbean was the documented center of Epstein's trafficking operation.

Candace Owens highlighted the case publicly, noting: "He was a crypto millionaire named Nikolai Mushegian. He died in a mysterious drowning in Puerto Rico — 4 hours after sending this tweet. He was fully clothed and had his wallet on him."
