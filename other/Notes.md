## Notes on Deaths Mentioned Across These Sources

Whitney Webb's *One Nation Under Blackmail* extensively documents:
- Robert Maxwell's 1991 death and PROMIS/Mossad intelligence operations
- Danny Casolaro's 1991 death while investigating the same PROMIS software operation
- The intelligence infrastructure connecting Epstein to state-level blackmail operations

Alana Goodman's *A Convenient Death* focuses on:
- The specific circumstances of Epstein's death in MCC
- Guard negligence, camera failures, and contradictions in the official narrative

Shaun Attwood's *Who Killed Epstein?* covers:
- Multiple suspicious deaths in the Epstein orbit
- The pattern of witnesses dying before testimony

Julie Brown's *Perversion of Justice* documents:
- Lead detective Joe Recarey's unexpected death at 50
- The systematic obstruction of the original Palm Beach investigation
- Alexander Acosta's sweetheart plea deal

Bradley Edwards' *Relentless Pursuit* covers:
- Alfredo Rodriguez's attempt to expose Epstein's black book and subsequent imprisonment
- The legal battles to protect victims who later died
