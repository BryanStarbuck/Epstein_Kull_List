## Dark Money / Arabella Advisors Connection

Rewiring America originated as a project of the **Windward Fund**, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit managed by **Arabella Advisors**. The Windward Fund is part of what critics call a "dark money" network:

- Windward Fund was created in February 2015 with $5.25 million from an unknown source
- It serves as a fiscal sponsor for numerous climate and energy projects
- It does not disclose its donors
- Rewiring America spun off as an independent 501(c)(3) circa 2023
- **Ari Matusiak** is CEO of both Rewiring America and founder of Power Forward Communities

## Summary of Red Flags Cited by Critics

1. **$100 revenue to $2 billion grant:** Organization had only $100 in reported revenue when it received one of the largest EPA grants ever
2. **Personnel overlap:** Same CEO (Matusiak) runs both the grant recipient (PFC) and the largest sub-recipient (Rewiring America)
3. **Rewiring America received majority of funds:** Over $5M of the ~$9.5M disbursed went to Rewiring America's affiliate
4. **Part-time CEO compensation:** Mayopoulos earned $206,250 for 20 hours/week at PFC plus $181,121 consulting from Rewiring America
5. **Going concern flag:** ProPublica flagged the 2024 990 with a going concern warning
6. **Dark money origins:** Rewiring America originated from Arabella Advisors' Windward Fund network
7. **Zero full-time employees:** Despite $2 billion in assets, PFC reported zero full-time employees
8. **Stacey Abrams' role downplayed:** Initially credited as "pivotal," later characterized as merely "alleged" in court filings
