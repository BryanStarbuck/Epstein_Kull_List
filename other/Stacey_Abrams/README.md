# Stacey Abrams & Power Forward Communities

**A $2 billion EPA grant to a nonprofit with $100 in revenue — political favoritism, dark money, and the revolving door.**

---

## Overview

Stacey Yvonne Abrams is a prominent Democratic politician, lawyer, voting rights activist, and author who served in the Georgia House of Representatives (2007–2017) and was the Democratic nominee for governor of Georgia in 2018 and 2022.

In March 2023, Abrams joined **Rewiring America** as Senior Counsel. That summer, she and Rewiring America CEO **Ari Matusiak** assembled a coalition called **Power Forward Communities (PFC)**, which was formally announced in October 2023. Six months later — with only **$100 in reported revenue** — PFC received a **$2 billion grant** from the Biden EPA under the Inflation Reduction Act's Greenhouse Gas Reduction Fund.

The grant, the money's handling, and the web of interconnected nonprofits surrounding it have become the subject of congressional investigations, federal lawsuits, and ongoing litigation.

## Why This Matters

1. A nonprofit with **$100 in revenue** received **$2 billion** — a grant 20 million times its reported income
2. The **same person** (Ari Matusiak) is CEO of both the grant recipient (PFC) and the largest sub-recipient (Rewiring America)
3. Over **half of all disbursed funds** ($5M of $9.5M) went to Rewiring America's affiliate
4. PFC reported **zero full-time employees** despite $2B in assets
5. Rewiring America originated from the **Arabella Advisors "dark money" network** (Windward Fund)
6. Two additional Abrams-founded nonprofits (Fair Count, SEAP) were PFC coalition partners
7. Abrams' other nonprofits have histories of **financial irregularities** (Fair Fight's $20M legal fee controversy, New Georgia Project's record fine and dissolution)
8. The Biden EPA allegedly **parked billions at Citibank** to limit incoming administration oversight
9. A PFC director initially said Abrams played **"a pivotal role"** in securing the grant — PFC later called her connection merely **"alleged"** in court filings

## Patterns Worth Noting

### The Arabella Advisors Network
Rewiring America originated as a project of the **Windward Fund**, a 501(c)(3) managed by **Arabella Advisors** — a consulting firm that manages a network of nonprofits described by critics as a "dark money" operation. The Windward Fund was created in 2015 with $5.25 million from an unknown source and does not disclose its donors.

### The Revolving Door
- Biden administration officials reportedly introduced Abrams to Rewiring America
- **Jahi Wise**, the Biden-era GGRF acting director, oversaw a separate $5 billion grant to his **former employer** (Coalition for Green Capital) without recusing himself
- A Coalition for Green Capital policy director left to work at Biden's Climate Policy Office on GGRF implementation, then became an EPA political appointee

### Abrams Nonprofit Pattern
