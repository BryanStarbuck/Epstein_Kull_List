### How She Was Described — Then and Now

- **Initially:** A PFC director stated Abrams played **"a pivotal role"** in securing the $2 billion EPA grant
- **Later:** In court filings, PFC characterized her connection as merely **"alleged"**
- **Podcast evidence:** Rewiring America CEO Ari Matusiak stated on a podcast with Abrams that they "put together a coalition to apply for grants from Greenhouse Gas Reduction Fund" in summer 2023
- **Her own words (Oct 15, 2023):** "Thrilled to be part of @rewiringamerica and the Power Forward Communities coalition. This is how we expand access to clean energy."

### How She Was Introduced to Rewiring America

According to RealClearInvestigations (Paul Sperry), Biden administration officials reportedly introduced Abrams to Rewiring America. She was hired to "help pitch the federal project."

## The Pattern Across Abrams' Nonprofits

1. **Fair Fight Action** — $20M+ in legal fees to a friend's firm for a losing lawsuit; ended in debt
2. **New Georgia Project** — Record fine, 16 campaign finance violations, dissolved under congressional scrutiny
3. **Rewiring America/PFC** — Senior Counsel at organization whose CEO also ran the $2B grant recipient; role downplayed when politically inconvenient
4. **Fair Count & SEAP** — Both folded into PFC coalition, expanding Abrams' network reach into the $2B grant
