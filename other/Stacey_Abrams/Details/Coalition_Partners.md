## The Core Conflict of Interest: A Closed Loop

The allegation is straightforward:

1. **Matusiak helped draft the IRA provisions** creating the $27B GGRF — Sen. Martin Heinrich confirmed: "An enormous amount of the work that Rewiring America did ended up in the climate bill"
2. **Matusiak then founded PFC** to apply for grants from that same fund
3. **PFC's board is stacked** with leaders of the same organizations that receive PFC funds
4. **Both co-chairs** (Donovan and Matusiak) simultaneously run organizations that are PFC sub-recipients
5. **A senior EPA official** (Jahi Wise) who oversaw GGRF awards previously worked for another grant recipient
6. **Stacey Abrams**, a major Democratic political figure, was brought in to help build and promote the coalition
7. **No lobbying registration:** Despite acknowledged influence on the IRA, no federal lobbying records show Matusiak or Rewiring America registered as lobbyists

**The irony:** The four established partners (Enterprise, Habitat, LISC, United Way) have combined assets of over **$3.2 billion** and combined annual revenues exceeding **$925 million**. These are legitimate, large-scale nonprofits with decades of track records. Yet the **smallest, newest partner** (Rewiring America, founded 2020, dark money origins) received **over half** of all disbursed funds — and its CEO is the one who created PFC.

### Why Rewiring America Matters Most

- **Ari Matusiak** is CEO of Rewiring America AND founder/co-chair of PFC — the entity that received the $2B grant
- Rewiring America's affiliate received **$5M of $9.5M** disbursed — more than all other partners combined
- Matusiak reportedly **helped draft the IRA legislation** that created the GGRF, then created PFC to apply for those grants
- Rewiring America originated from the **Windward Fund**, part of the Arabella Advisors network — described by critics as the largest dark money operation in American politics
- **Stacey Abrams** served as Senior Counsel (March 2023 – Dec 2024)
- Abrams' nonprofits **Fair Count** and **SEAP** were listed as PFC pipeline partners

### Rewiring America's Role in PFC

Rewiring America is the **primary programmatic partner** for single-family, owner-occupied home electrification. It:
- Designs and deploys electrification programs for low-to-moderate income homeowners
- Serves as "contracting counterparty" for owner-occupied programs
- Selects contractors and manages implementation
