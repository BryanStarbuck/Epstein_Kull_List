## Overview

Power Forward Communities (PFC) is a nonprofit coalition formed in October 2023, allegedly to deploy capital for affordable residential decarbonization in low-income and disadvantaged communities. The coalition was assembled by **Ari Matusiak** (CEO of Rewiring America) and **Stacey Abrams** (Senior Counsel at Rewiring America) during the summer of 2023, according to Matusiak's own podcast statements.

Within six months of formation — and with only **$100 in reported revenue** — PFC was awarded a **$2 billion grant** from the Biden EPA's Greenhouse Gas Reduction Fund.

## Dark Money Origins — The Arabella Advisors Connection

Rewiring America — PFC's largest partner and sub-recipient — originated as a project of the **Windward Fund**, a 501(c)(3) managed by **Arabella Advisors**.

| Fact | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **What is Arabella Advisors?** | A consulting firm that manages a network of nonprofits described by critics as a "dark money" operation |
| **What is the Windward Fund?** | A 501(c)(3) created in February 2015 with $5.25 million from an unknown source |
| **Donor Disclosure** | The Windward Fund does not disclose its donors |
| **Rewiring America's Origin** | Launched as a Windward Fund project circa 2020 |
| **Spin-Off** | Rewiring America became an independent 501(c)(3) circa 2023 |

## The "Going Concern" Warning

ProPublica flagged PFC's 2024 Form 990 with a "going concern" — indicating **"substantial doubt about this organization's ability to meet its financial obligations."**

This is notable because:
- PFC had $2 billion in cash at Citibank
- But those funds were frozen by the Trump EPA in February 2025
- PFC's own operations generated $0 in independent revenue
- Without the EPA grant, PFC has no revenue source
