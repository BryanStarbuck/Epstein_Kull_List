# UAP Podcasts — Disclosure, Whistleblowers, and Suspicious Deaths

This page catalogs podcasts that cover UAP/UFO disclosure, government secrecy, whistleblower testimony, suspicious deaths of researchers, classified programs, and reverse-engineering allegations. Many of these shows have featured people profiled in this project as guests, or have dedicated episodes to investigating their deaths.

Podcasts are organized by category. Cross-links to individual profiles in this project are provided throughout.

### The Joe Rogan Experience

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Host** | Joe Rogan |
| **Platform** | Spotify (exclusive) |
| **Started** | 2009 |

The largest podcast in the world, with episodes regularly reaching tens of millions of viewers. Rogan's long-form interview format (often 3+ hours) allows for deep dives into UAP topics that mainstream media rarely permits. Several episodes have been watershed moments for bringing UAP disclosure to mass audiences.

**Key UAP Episodes:**
- **#1315 — [Bob Lazar](Details/Bob_Lazar.md) & Jeremy Corbell**: Lazar's detailed account of alleged reverse-engineering work on alien spacecraft at S-4 near Area 51, describing propulsion systems that manipulated gravity. Over 40 million views. This episode brought Lazar's story to a new generation and remains one of the most-viewed UAP episodes in podcast history.
- **#2065 — [David Grusch](Details/David_Grusch.md)**: The UAP whistleblower discussed alleged government crash retrieval programs and reverse-engineering efforts dating back nearly a century. Grusch described the secrecy apparatus and the risks whistleblowers face, including his statement that people have been harmed or killed.
- **#2194 — [Lue Elizondo](Details/Lue_Elizondo.md)**: The former head of AATIP discussed the Pentagon's UFO program, his book *Imminent*, and the cover-up. Aired August 23, 2024.
- **#1361 — Commander David Fravor**: The Navy pilot who encountered the Tic Tac UAP off the USS Nimitz in 2004 described the encounter in detail. One of the most-watched UAP interviews ever.
- **#1574 — Jacques Vallee & James Fox**: Discussion of UAP research history, including deaths of researchers.
- **Multiple episodes with [George Knapp](Details/George_Knapp.md)** discussing Skinwalker Ranch, [Bob Lazar](Details/Bob_Lazar.md), and government secrecy.

**Profiled People Covered:** [Bob Lazar](Details/Bob_Lazar.md), [David Grusch](Details/David_Grusch.md), [Lue Elizondo](Details/Lue_Elizondo.md), [George Knapp](Details/George_Knapp.md)

## Patterns Across Podcasts

**The "Grusch Effect"**: [David Grusch](Details/David_Grusch.md)'s July 2023 congressional testimony created an explosion of UAP podcast content. Virtually every show listed here produced multiple episodes analyzing his claims. His statement that he had "personal knowledge" of people being harmed or killed to conceal UAP technology is directly relevant to this project's scope and is referenced across dozens of episodes.

**Whistleblower Pipeline**: Several podcasts serve as the first stop for new whistleblowers coming forward. Weaponized, NewsNation (Ross Coulthart), and Vetted have all broken stories from new sources. The Danny Jones Podcast and Shawn Ryan Show reach audiences outside the traditional UAP community, expanding the reach of disclosure.

**Suspicious Death Coverage**: The deaths of [Phil Schneider](Details/Phil_Schneider.md) and [Mark McCandlish](Details/Mark_McCandlish.md) are the most frequently covered in podcast format. Schneider's case has been the subject of dedicated episodes on at least five major shows. McCandlish's death — occurring in 2021, during the modern podcast era — received real-time coverage and analysis.

**The Lue Elizondo Tour**: Following the August 2024 publication of his book *Imminent*, [Lue Elizondo](Details/Lue_Elizondo.md) appeared on more than a dozen major podcasts within a few months, from Joe Rogan to The Daily Show to Diary of a CEO. This represents the most significant single-person media campaign in UAP disclosure history and brought the topic to mainstream audiences who had never previously engaged with it.

**George Knapp as the Common Thread**: [George Knapp](Details/George_Knapp.md) connects the old era and the new. He broke the [Bob Lazar](Details/Bob_Lazar.md) story in 1989, hosted Coast to Coast AM, and now co-hosts Weaponized. His journalism spans the entire modern UAP era and he has interviewed or investigated many of the people profiled in this project.

**Last Interviews Before Death**: Several people profiled in this project gave what turned out to be their last public statements on podcasts or radio shows. [Phil Schneider](Details/Phil_Schneider.md)'s 1995 lectures, [William Cooper](Details/William_Cooper.md)'s Hour of the Time broadcasts, and [Fred Bell](Details/Fred_Bell.md)'s Coast to Coast AM appearance are all examples. These recordings serve as primary source material for understanding what these individuals claimed to know.

## How to Use This List

These podcasts serve as both primary sources (when they feature interviews with profiled individuals) and secondary sources (when hosts investigate cases). When expanding profiles in [Details/](Details/):

1. Check if the person appeared as a podcast guest — their own words are valuable primary sources
2. Note specific episode numbers and dates for sourcing
3. Cross-reference claims across multiple shows
4. Pay attention to "last interviews" — some profiled individuals gave their final public statements on podcasts before dying
5. Look for corroboration: when multiple independent shows investigate the same case and reach similar conclusions, that strengthens the evidence
