## Circumstances of Death

On June 22, 2025, Monica Jacinto Reza was last seen at approximately 9:00 a.m. near the trailhead on Angeles Crest Highway near Mount Waterman in the Angeles National Forest. She was hiking alone.

When she failed to return, a search operation was launched involving teams from multiple counties, including San Bernardino County and Tulare County, as well as helicopter support and K-9 units. The search continued for over a week before being called off. The Los Angeles County Sheriff's Department's Homicide Bureau Missing Persons Unit continued the investigation.

The Angeles National Forest is a large, rugged wilderness area with steep terrain, and hikers go missing there with some regularity due to falls, dehydration, exposure, and difficult navigation.
