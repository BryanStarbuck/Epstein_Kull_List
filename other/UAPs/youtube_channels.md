- **Subject:** [Phil Schneider](Details/Phil_Schneider.md)
- **Description:** Multiple YouTube channels host recordings of [Phil Schneider](Details/Phil_Schneider.md)'s lectures from 1995-1996, in which he claimed to have worked building Deep Underground Military Bases (DUMBs) for the U.S. government. Schneider alleged he was involved in the construction of over 131 underground bases, had encounters with extraterrestrial biological entities, and worked with exotic materials. His last known lecture recording was made in Denver in November 1995 — this video was "lost" until late August 1996. In January 1996, Schneider was found dead in his apartment, strangled with a rubber catheter tube. His death was ruled a suicide, a determination disputed by his family and supporters. Some of his lecture videos have been removed from platforms over the years but continue to resurface.
- **Notable Videos:**
  - Denver November 1995 lecture — last known recording before his death
  - "The Underground: A Hidden Reality and the True Story of Phil Schneider" (2012 documentary, also on Apple TV)
  - Multiple lecture recordings covering Dulce Base, DUMBs, and alien encounters
- **Profiled People Covered:** [Phil Schneider](Details/Phil_Schneider.md)

### Stanton Friedman Lectures (Various Channels)
- **Subject:** [Stanton Friedman](Details/Stanton_Friedman.md)
- **Description:** Nuclear physicist [Stanton Friedman](Details/Stanton_Friedman.md) gave lectures at over 600 colleges and to more than 100 professional groups across 50 states, 10 provinces, and 19 countries. His signature lecture "Flying Saucers ARE Real" became legendary in the UFO research community. Many of these lectures have been preserved on YouTube across various channels and the Internet Archive. Friedman was a pioneer in UFO research, the original civilian investigator of the Roswell incident, and a frequent guest on Larry King Live. He died in 2019 at age 84, leaving behind a vast archive that filled five cargo vans, now housed at the Provincial Archives of New Brunswick, Canada. The Fredericton Region Museum maintains a permanent Stanton Friedman exhibit.
- **Notable Videos:**
  - "Flying Saucers ARE Real" lecture recordings
  - Debates with skeptics and scientists
  - Roswell investigation presentations
  - Analysis of MJ-12 documents and [James Forrestal](Details/James_Forrestal.md)'s connection
  - Appearances in UFO documentaries and news programs
  - Multiple appearances on Larry King Live
- **Profiled People Covered:** [Stanton Friedman](Details/Stanton_Friedman.md), [James Forrestal](Details/James_Forrestal.md)

### William Cooper Lectures & Hour of the Time (Various Channels)
- **Subject:** [William Cooper](Details/William_Cooper.md)
- **Description:** [William Milton Cooper](Details/William_Cooper.md), former U.S. Naval Intelligence briefing team member and author of *Behold a Pale Horse*, gave numerous lectures in the early 1990s claiming knowledge of government UFO cover-ups, secret societies, and classified programs. His shortwave radio show "The Hour of the Time" reached millions of listeners. Cooper was shot and killed by Apache County sheriff's deputies on November 5, 2001, in what officials called an attempt to serve an arrest warrant. His supporters dispute the circumstances of his death. Many of his lectures and radio broadcasts have been preserved on YouTube across various archive channels.
- **Notable Videos:**
  - Lectures on government UFO secrecy and alleged alien treaties
  - "The Hour of the Time" radio broadcast recordings
  - Presentations on MJ-12 and secret government operations
  - Lectures connecting intelligence operations to UAP cover-ups
- **Profiled People Covered:** [William Cooper](Details/William_Cooper.md)

## Patterns Across Channels

Several patterns emerge when examining YouTube coverage of UAP-related deaths and suppression:

**Coverage concentration on recent whistleblowers.** Channels overwhelmingly focus on living figures like [David Grusch](Details/David_Grusch.md), [Lue Elizondo](Details/Lue_Elizondo.md), and [Ryan Graves](Details/Ryan_Graves.md). Historical deaths like [Phil Schneider](Details/Phil_Schneider.md), [James Forrestal](Details/James_Forrestal.md), and [William Cooper](Details/William_Cooper.md) receive less frequent but more in-depth treatment, typically in documentary-style long-form content.

**The GEC-Marconi scientist deaths remain underexplored.** Despite being one of the most striking clusters — 25 defense scientists dying in 8 years during the SDI/"Star Wars" era — few major channels have produced comprehensive coverage of the Marconi deaths. Richard Dolan, The Why Files, The Black Vault, and Cristina Gomez are notable exceptions.

**Suppressed energy technology is increasingly linked to UAP.** Channels like Dr. Steven Greer's and Richard Dolan's explicitly connect zero-point energy and exotic propulsion suppression to UAP reverse-engineering cover-ups. Greer's *The Lost Century* (2023) specifically documents the energy suppression angle. This connects to people documented in both this directory and the sibling [Zero Point Energy](../Zero_Point_Energy/) directory.

**Platform vulnerability.** Multiple channels report that UAP content, particularly content about suspicious deaths and government cover-ups, faces demonetization, algorithmic suppression, or removal. [Phil Schneider](Details/Phil_Schneider.md) lecture recordings have been repeatedly removed and re-uploaded across different channels. Eyes on Cinema maintains a presence on Odysee and BitChute as backup platforms.

**Congressional testimony as turning point.** The July 2023 Grusch hearing, broadcast live on C-SPAN and covered by NewsNation, appears to have legitimized UAP coverage for mainstream channels. Before 2023, serious UAP coverage on YouTube was concentrated in specialized channels. After 2023, major platforms (Joe Rogan, Diary of a CEO, Yes Theory, mainstream news) began regularly featuring UAP whistleblowers and researchers.

**First-person testimony before death.** Several individuals documented in this project gave their final public statements on YouTube channels before dying. [Phil Schneider](Details/Phil_Schneider.md)'s Denver lecture (November 1995), [Boyd Bushman](Details/Boyd_Bushman.md)'s deathbed confession (2014), and [Mark McCandlish](Details/Mark_McCandlish.md)'s Disclosure Project testimony are examples of testimony preserved on YouTube by individuals who later died under suspicious circumstances.

## How to Use This Guide

- **For research into specific deaths:** Start with Richard Dolan, The Why Files, or Eyes on Cinema for historical cases. Check The Black Vault for declassified documents.
- **For current disclosure developments:** Follow Weaponized, Need to Know, NewsNation, and C-SPAN hearing recordings.
- **For technical analysis of UAP evidence:** Chris Lehto and Vetted provide evidence-based analysis from military and investigative perspectives.
- **For comprehensive long-form investigations:** UAP Gerb and The Why Files produce documentary-length deep dives on single topics.
- **For mainstream-accessible introductions:** Joe Rogan interviews and NewsNation coverage are the most accessible entry points for people new to the topic.
- **For archival and historical footage:** Eyes on Cinema is the primary resource for rare, vintage UFO footage and broadcasts.
- **For legal and legislative analysis:** New Paradigm Institute and Disclosure Team focus on the legal and congressional dimensions.
- **For first-person testimony:** Check lecture archives of [Phil Schneider](Details/Phil_Schneider.md), [William Cooper](Details/William_Cooper.md), [Stanton Friedman](Details/Stanton_Friedman.md), and [Boyd Bushman](Details/Boyd_Bushman.md) for their own words before their deaths.
- **For sourcing profiles in Details/:** YouTube interviews count as primary sources when the profiled person is speaking. Note specific video titles and dates.
