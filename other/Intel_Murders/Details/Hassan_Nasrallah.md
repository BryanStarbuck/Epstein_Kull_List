## Assessment: CONFIRMED

The killing of Hassan Nasrallah was openly claimed by the Israeli Defense Forces on 28 September 2024. Israeli leaders reportedly tracked his location for months before authorizing the strike. This was the highest-profile Israeli targeted killing in decades, eliminating the leader of Iran's most important proxy force during escalating regional conflict.

## Circumstances of Death

On the evening of 27 September 2024, the Israeli Air Force launched a massive strike on Hezbollah's underground headquarters complex in the Haret Hreik neighborhood of the Dahieh suburb in southern Beirut. Israeli F-15I fighters reportedly dropped over 80 bombs, including bunker-busting munitions, targeting a command center located approximately 60 feet (18 meters) underground beneath residential buildings. Nasrallah was reportedly meeting with senior Hezbollah commanders at the time. The IDF announced his death the following day, and his body was recovered from the rubble on 29 September. The strike killed at least 33 people and injured over 195, including civilians. Among the dead were Ali Karaki, commander of Hezbollah's Southern Front, and Abbas Nilforoushan, deputy commander of Iran's Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps.

## Background

Hassan Nasrallah was born on 31 August 1960 in Bourj Hammoud, an eastern suburb of Beirut, the ninth of ten children in a Shia family. His father was a fruit and vegetable seller originally from southern Lebanon. Nasrallah studied at Shia seminaries in Baalbek and later in Najaf, Iraq. He briefly joined the Amal Movement before defecting in 1982 to help form Hezbollah following Israel's invasion of Lebanon. He became Hezbollah's Secretary-General in 1992 after his predecessor, Abbas al-Musawi, was killed by an Israeli airstrike. Under Nasrallah's leadership, Hezbollah grew into the most powerful non-state military force in the Middle East, with an arsenal reportedly exceeding 150,000 rockets and missiles. The United States, European Union, and several Arab states designated Hezbollah as a terrorist organization.
