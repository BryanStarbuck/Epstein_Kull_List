## Assessment: CONFIRMED

The assassination of Mahmoud Hamshari was carried out by Mossad as part of Operation Wrath of God (Operation Bayonet), the covert assassination campaign authorized by Israeli Prime Minister Golda Meir in autumn 1972 to kill individuals accused of involvement in the Munich Olympics massacre. Israeli minister of information Aharon Yariv was reportedly in charge of the assassination plans targeting Hamshari. The operation is well-documented in multiple intelligence histories.

## Circumstances of Death

On December 8, 1972, a Mossad agent posing as an Italian journalist contacted Hamshari and arranged to meet him. This earlier contact had allowed the agent to lure Hamshari away from his Paris apartment long enough for a Mossad demolition team to enter and install a small explosive device underneath his desk telephone. On December 8, the agent called Hamshari's apartment and asked to confirm he was speaking to Hamshari. After Hamshari identified himself, the agent signaled to colleagues who sent a detonation signal down the telephone line, triggering the bomb. The explosion severely wounded Hamshari. Despite his injuries, he remained conscious long enough to tell French detectives what had happened. He was rushed to a Paris hospital but succumbed to his wounds on January 9, 1973, approximately one month later. His young daughter, who was also in the apartment, survived.

## Background

Hamshari served as the official representative of the Palestine Liberation Organization (PLO) in France, functioning as the PLO's de facto ambassador in Paris. Israel accused him of involvement in the Black September network and of helping to plan the Munich Olympics attack of September 5, 1972, in which 11 Israeli athletes were kidnapped and killed. Hamshari was one of the most prominent Palestinian political figures in Europe and used his Paris position to advocate for Palestinian statehood and build diplomatic relationships with European governments and leftist movements.

## Intelligence Connections

* Mossad carried out the assassination as part of Operation Wrath of God, authorized by PM Golda Meir
* Israeli minister Aharon Yariv oversaw the assassination planning
* A Mossad agent spent weeks cultivating contact with Hamshari under false identity as an Italian journalist
* A Mossad demolition team broke into Hamshari's apartment to plant the explosive device
* The phone-triggered bomb represented sophisticated remote detonation technology for 1972
* The operation was conducted on French soil without French government knowledge or consent
* According to academic research, Mossad relied on cooperation with foreign intelligence contacts to organize logistics for the killing campaign

## Why This Death Raises Questions

- The bombing of a residential apartment in Paris endangered civilians, including Hamshari's young daughter who was present
- The operation was conducted on the sovereign territory of France, a major Western ally of Israel
- Hamshari's actual role in the Munich massacre planning has been disputed by some sources — he may have been targeted more for his political role than for operational involvement
- The phone-bomb technique — requiring entry into a private apartment and installation of hidden explosives — demonstrated a willingness to conduct complex covert operations in allied Western capitals
- The operation was one of the earliest in a campaign that would continue for over 20 years
- French authorities were unable or unwilling to identify and prosecute the perpetrators
