## Assessment: CONFIRMED

Atef Bseiso's killing is now widely attributed to Mossad as part of Operation Wrath of God, the Israeli campaign to assassinate those believed responsible for the 1972 Munich Olympics massacre. Bseiso was reportedly the only Wrath of God target later assessed as having a direct operational connection to the Munich attack. At the time, Israel denied involvement — Prime Minister Yitzhak Shamir's spokesman called the claims "completely ridiculous."

## Circumstances of Death

On the night of 8 June 1992, Atef Bseiso was returning to his Paris hotel around 1:00 a.m. after dinner with Lebanese friends. As he approached the entrance, two men armed with silencer-equipped pistols shot him multiple times at close range, killing him. The gunmen fled the scene and were never apprehended by French authorities. The assassination was carried out with the hallmarks of a professional intelligence operation — precise timing, suppressed weapons, and clean escape.

## Background

Atef Bseiso was a rising star within the PLO hierarchy by the early 1990s. He served as the PLO's chief liaison officer with foreign intelligence agencies, maintaining contacts with the CIA, German intelligence (BND), and France's Direction de la Surveillance du Territoire (DST). This role placed him at the intersection of Palestinian politics and Western espionage, making him both valuable and vulnerable. According to Israeli intelligence assessments, Bseiso had been directly involved in planning the 1972 Munich Olympics massacre in which 11 Israeli athletes and coaches were killed by the Black September organization. This alleged connection to Munich made him a target of Operation Wrath of God, the long-running Mossad campaign authorized by Prime Minister Golda Meir to hunt down those responsible.

## Intelligence Connections

* Bseiso served as the PLO's official liaison with the CIA, BND (German intelligence), and DST (French intelligence)
* Israel's Mossad identified him as directly connected to the 1972 Munich Olympics massacre planning
* His killing was part of Operation Wrath of God, which had been running intermittently since 1972
* According to journalist Ronen Bergman, Bseiso was the only Wrath of God target later confirmed as having had a direct operational role in Munich
* Some analysts argued that the real motivation was disrupting the PLO's intelligence relationships with Western services ahead of the Oslo negotiations

## Why This Death Raises Questions

- The assassination was carried out on French soil, straining Israeli-French relations
- Bseiso's role as CIA liaison meant his killing potentially disrupted American intelligence operations
- Some questioned whether the Munich connection was the real motive, or whether Israel sought to eliminate the PLO's Western intelligence contacts before peace negotiations
- The killing came 20 years after Munich, raising questions about whether vengeance or current strategic interests drove the operation
- French authorities never identified or arrested the killers despite the attack occurring in central Paris
