## Assessment: NOT SUSPICIOUS — MISATTRIBUTION

Monica Jacinto Reza has been cited in some conspiracy theory compilations as being "involved in anti-gravity research and fusion/plasma science" who died or went missing suspiciously. However, extensive research has found **no credible evidence** connecting her to any scientific research, academic publications, institutional affiliations, anti-gravity work, fusion research, or plasma science. She does not appear in any academic databases, university faculty pages, or scientific publication records. Her death appears to be a hiking accident/disappearance in Angeles National Forest, not connected to suppressed technology. The claims appear to be fabricated or a case of mistaken identity.

## Circumstances of Death

On the morning of June 22, 2025, Monica Reza set out on a hike near the Mount Waterman area at 6000 Foot Gate on Angeles Crest Highway. She was last seen at approximately 9:10 a.m.

When she did not return, a large multi-agency search was launched involving teams from San Diego to Tulare County, conducted over at least six days. Her body was eventually recovered. She received a green burial in Angeles National Forest.

## Why This Entry Exists

Monica Jacinto Reza appears on lists circulated on X (formerly Twitter) and other platforms claiming to document scientists and researchers "murdered or silenced to prevent dissemination of breakthroughs in zero-point energy, free energy, cold fusion, anti-gravity propulsion, or related UAP technologies."

After thorough web research, no evidence was found to support the claim that Reza was a scientist of any kind. Her inclusion in such lists appears to be an error — possibly confusion with another person, or fabrication. This entry exists to document the claim and its debunking.
