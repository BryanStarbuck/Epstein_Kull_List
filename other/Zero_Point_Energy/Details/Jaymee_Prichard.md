## Assessment: SUSPICIOUS — PART OF AFRL CLUSTER

Jaymee Prichard was a victim of murder — killed by strangulation by her husband before he went on to kill his AFRL colleague [1st Lt. Jaime Gustitus](Jaime_Gustitus.md). She is documented here as part of the pattern of three Wright-Patterson employees dead in one night during a nine-month period in which nine AFRL-connected individuals died or disappeared.

## Circumstances of Death

On October 25, 2025, Jaymee was killed by strangulation by her husband Jacob Prichard at an unknown time. He placed her body in the trunk of their vehicle. He then drove to Sugarcreek Township, broke into the apartment of 1st Lt. Gustitus, and shot her to death. He subsequently drove to the West Milton Municipal Building, opened the trunk so that officers would find Jaymee, and killed himself.

West Milton Police Chief Doyle Wright confirmed that the couple's three children were later located and are safe.
