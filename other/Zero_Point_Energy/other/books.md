# Books on Suppressed Energy Breakthroughs

Books documenting the suppression, destruction, and mysterious deaths of inventors and researchers working on advanced energy technologies. Organized by topic area. Where a book directly connects to a person profiled in this project, their name links to their detail page.

## Key Compilations and Reference Works

These are not traditional books but serve as foundational references for the field:

- **Gary Vesperman's *Energy Invention Suppression Cases*** — 95+ documented cases. Available online. The single most comprehensive catalog of energy suppression incidents. Many profiles in this project cite it as a primary source.
- **Rex Research (rexresearch.com)** — Online archive of patents and technical descriptions for suppressed inventions including [Tom Ogle](../Details/Tom_Ogle.md), [Floyd Sweet](../Details/Floyd_Sweet.md), [Paul Brown](../Details/Paul_Brown.md), [Andrija Puharich](../Details/Andrija_Puharich.md), and [Bruce DePalma](../Details/Bruce_DePalma.md).
- **Infinite Energy Magazine** (1995–2024) — Founded by [Eugene Mallove](../Details/Eugene_Mallove.md). Published 167 issues covering cold fusion, LENR, and new energy research. Final issue: March–June 2024.
