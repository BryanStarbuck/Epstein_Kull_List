## Why New York Matters

Epstein's Manhattan townhouse at 9 East 71st Street — one of the largest private residences in Manhattan — served as a primary base for alleged trafficking and blackmail operations. The property was reportedly wired with hidden cameras. New York was also the hub for Epstein's financial operations, his connections to [Deutsche Bank](../groups/deutsche_bank.md) and [JPMorgan Chase](../groups/jpmorgan_chase.md), and the modeling industry pipeline through agencies like [MC2](../groups/mc2_model_management.md) and [Elite Model Management](../groups/elite_model_management.md).

Epstein died at the Metropolitan Correctional Center in Lower Manhattan — a federal jail that had not had an inmate suicide in 21 years before his death.
